Auto Rendering 2d editor

Draw a image with 4 colors and a depth map. The autorender will render a shaded image of it.

Effects

Because of the limited amount of colors, the presence of the depth map and the small resolution, visual effects can be easily applied to the picture without breaking the shading. The differents regions of the picture are easily identifiable by their color id. The depth map can be used to create a 3d effect.

Shading

The renderer support: directionnal shadows (bling-phong like), ambient occlusion, light emission (bloom), outline, smoothing, reflectiveness (bling-phong like).

Help

Leave the mouse on a button to get a tooltip that describe its function.
